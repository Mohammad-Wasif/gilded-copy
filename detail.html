Traditional Craftsmanship

Our Gold Zari thread is manufactured using centuries-old techniques refined for modern high-tension embroidery. Each strand features a core of high-strength synthetic silk wrapped in a delicate foil of 24k gold-plated silver.

Specifically designed for Zardosi and Aari work, this A-Grade thread ensures zero tarnishing and maintains its brilliant luster even after decades of wear on heirloom pieces.
